University Overview

APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (initially Kerala Technological University), a State Government University has come into existence on May 21, 2014 with an aim to give leadership to the technology related policy formulation and Engineering Planning for the State.

It also emphasizes to improve the academic standards of the Graduate, Post Graduate and Research Programmes in Engineering Science, Technology and Management and regulate the academic standards of all colleges affiliated to the University.

Our Vision

To be an institution of global excellence in engineering education and research, producing professionals who are technologically adept, ethically grounded, and socially responsible.

Our Mission

To formulate policies, plan and regulate engineering education, and promote research that addresses the technological needs of the society.
